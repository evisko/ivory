We've got what you need!

IVORY Studio is very well known in the minds of the music community since the beginning of its operation, it mainly plays field of modern music, then film and orchestral...

IVORY Studio was founded in 1995 based in Bratislava, and now has a permanent establishment in Hubeného 7 in the quiet part of Bratislava Krasňany with excellent transportation access and plenty of parking spaces.

Our Services

The study IVORY can handle any digital sound quality in 2.0 format, up to 5.1 and all types of audio recordings and transcripts of known carriers. Production provides professional team with years of experience in the field of dubbing, sound recording and offers its high-quality treatment at all stages of production from preparation to final mix.
